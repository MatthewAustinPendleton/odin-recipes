French Toast Without Milk

Description

This French toast without milk is perfect for those who like a more solid French toast. My son's friends really liked this and would ask for the recipe to take home for their moms to make! Serve with butter and syrup.

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tablespoon white or brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ½ teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • ¼ teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter
  • 4 slices white bread

Steps

  1. Gather the ingredients.
  2. Beat eggs in a wide mixing bowl; mix in sugar, vanilla extract, cinnamon, and nutmeg.
  3. Melt butter in a skillet over medium-low heat.
  4. Dip 2 slices of bread into egg mixture, ensuring both sides are covered; place in the skillet and cook until golden brown, about 4 minutes per side. Repeat with remaining 2 slices of bread. Serve warm.
